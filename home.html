WHO WE ARE?

about rulebook

Rulebook Company specializes in providing comprehensive and timely updates on trade venue rulebooks and fee schedules, addressing a critical need for financial institutions to stay compliant and informed in an ever-evolving regulatory landscape. Rulebook Company ensures that clients can navigate complex regulatory requirements with ease.

Our expertise lies in reviewing complex updates using modern technology such as Large Language Models to break down complex updates into actionable insights. This helps banks and businesses maintain compliance, mitigate risks, and optimize their operations in the financial industry.

SERVICES

what we serve

Rulebook Updates

  • Weekly summaries of rulebook changes for exchanges, MTFs (Multilateral Trading Facilities), and OTFs (Organized Trading Facilities).

  • Large language models monitor and distill complex updates into easy-to-understand summaries, ensuring clients are always informed.

Fee Schedule Updates

  • Timely updates on fee schedules, essential for accurate execution cost calculations.

  • Helps clients maintain compliance and avoid financial and reputational damage due to incorrect fee applications.

Innovation

technology solution

Rulebook Company utilizes proprietary modern technology and large language models to continuously monitor and summarize rulebook or fee schedule updates from various trade trading venues. These updates are analyzed and simplified into actionable insights delivered to clients’ inboxes weekly. The technology ensures that all relevant changes are highlighted through color-coded, side-by-side analysis, making it easy to identify text deletions, amendments, or additions.
